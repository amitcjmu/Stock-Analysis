# AI Modernize Migration Platform: Enterprise Product Roadmap
## 12-16 Month Strategic Plan

### Executive Summary
This roadmap transforms our AI-powered migration platform from a promising proof-of-concept into an enterprise-ready solution capable of generating $5M ARR in Year 1. We'll address critical operational risks, complete our agent ecosystem, and build the enterprise features necessary for market success.

**Key Strategic Priorities:**
1. **Eliminate single point of failure** through team scaling
2. **Complete the 17-agent vision** while maintaining architectural integrity
3. **Enterprise-grade stability and features** for production deployments
4. **Revenue acceleration** through partnerships and tiered pricing
